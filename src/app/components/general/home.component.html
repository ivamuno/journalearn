The most effective way to learn writing in another language

Take a while to write about your experiences and feelings. Do not waste your time over-thinking, use the most common vocabulary which you use daily basis. Then, share to obtain reviews from natives and learn.

Write

These are some of the benefits which you can get from writing about your livings in another language:

  • Practice with the verbal tense. What did I do yesterday?, What have I done today?, What will I do tomorrow?
  • Learn some new words which you uses normally.
  • You do not need previous preparation to start writing.

Reviews

No excuses, no fears, forget what you do not know. You do not need neither wasting your time searching in the dictionary nor reviewing if what you write is ok or not, a native person will do that for you! In that way you will not feel frustrated because of your lack of knowledge and the writing will be more fluent.

Collaborate and take part of the community reviewing journals in your own language.

Remember, whatever your write is public. You set the limits, if you are worried about privacy then you should not write about it.

Learn

Have a look at the reviews to complete the learning and improve you skills day by day.

Be humble and embrace corrections/advises, this is the best help you can have when your are learning.
